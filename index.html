PROJECT HIGHLIGHT: COUNTUP – The Bag Chasers Collection

Role: Lead Designer, Brand Strategist, Creative Director

When CountUpD sent me a post teasing his new clothing line, I took initiative. I turned that mockup into a full design blueprint, coordinated with overseas production, created visuals, and crafted the rollout—all before the first sample arrived. What I did: – Designed the COUNTUP logo, ‘Chase Racks’ tee, ‘Kid Counting Money’ tee – Led production through Motoma Industries in Pakistan – Styled lookbooks, AI-enhanced previews, Procreate artwork, sleeve appliqué placements – Directed photo shoots, managed revisions, and built social-ready content – Engaged independent manufacturing for custom prototypes and product lines This was a case study in how far I can push a brand visually and strategically, solo. I’m using COUNTUP to demonstrate how I build brands from scratch with no excuses. Again, no shortcuts taken. I dove completely into leveraging their brand identity to create a strong, cohesive, highly stylish product line that reflects their target market.
